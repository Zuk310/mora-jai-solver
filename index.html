Mora Jai Puzzle Solver

Guide

The objective of the Mora Jai Puzzle is to match the colors of the four corner tiles on the 3x3 grid with their respective corner colors.

Colors Mechanics:

  • Grey: Does nothing.
  • Black: Shifts its row right.
  • Green: Swaps with the opposite tile.
  • Pink: Rotates neighboring tiles clockwise.
  • Yellow: Swaps with the tile above.
  • Violet: Swaps with the tile below.
  • White: Turns adjacent gray tiles white; if none, turns itself gray.
  • Red: White becomes Black; Black becomes Red.
  • Orange: Becomes the majority color of its neighbors.
  • Blue: Triggers the center tile effect.
